About me:

I graduated with a chemical engineering bachelors from UC Berkeley in 2017. In UC Berkeley I had experience interning in a Sustainability non-profit where I helped convince many local businesses to decrease their food packaging waste by offering discounts for people with reusable containers. Additionally, I researched indoor air quality and investigated the cell metabolisms of Anaerobic Ammonia Oxidizing Organisms (Anammox). I am currently a PhD student in Harvard University. I study Environmental Engineering and work with the NASA GEOS-5 Earth System model. I am investigating how vertical resolution can improve the the simulation of transpacific pollution plumes.
